Interest in IT

Ideal Job: Software Engineer

The career path I would like to seek is that of software engineering. Software Engineers are involved in all steps of the software development process and this is something I am very passionate about, being across the entire project to ensure we get the best result possible for the end-user. A software engineer is responsible for many things including crucial input during the planning and prototyping stages to ensure requirements are defined. Software engineers are logical thinkers and responsible for the design, development and testing for accuracy and security, including the debugging before and after release. Along with this, they are responsible for the maintenance of the end result, consisting of improvements as well as identifying and removing problems.


What skills, qualifications and experience do Software Engineers need?

Software engineers require a Bachelor Degree in a related field such as:

  • Bachelor of Computer Science or Software Engineering

  • If an alternate degree is obtained such as a Bachelor of Information Technology, a graduate certificate in Computer Science or Software Engineering will provide the skill and knowledge to pursue software engineering, however, you still can without it.

  • To take your career even further, the individual can obtain a Masters of Engineering (software) or Computer Science

    Software engineers must have effective communication as they generally work within a team so they will need to poses excellent time management, be passionate and high-end user focus, and be logical and able to problem solving. Software engineers must be proficient in programming languages, development technologies and web development tools.

    Becoming a Software Engineer can take roughly 4+ years to acquire a junior level position.
